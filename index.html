About us

Alapuzha House boat Adventure

Welcome aboard! We specialize in offering unique and unforgettable houseboat experiences, where the beauty of nature meets the comfort of a floating home. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat on calm waters or an adventurous escape exploring hidden coves, our fleet of modern, fully-equipped houseboats provides the perfect setting for your getaway. With customizable packages, excellent service, and a commitment to creating lasting memories, we’re here to make your dream vacation on the water a reality.
